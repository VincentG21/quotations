Art

Without freedom, no art; art lives only on the restraints it imposes on itself, and dies of all others. - ALbert Camus
Some painters transform the sun into a yellow spot, others transform a yellow spot into the sun. - Pablo Picasso
All art is but imitation of nature. - Lucius Seneca

Education

The roots of education are bitter, but the fruit is sweet. - Aristotle
Education is the movement from darkness to light. - Allan Bloom
It is not enough to have a good mind;the main thing is to use it well. - Rene Descartes
Only the educated are free. - Epictetus

Passion

It is with our passions as it is with fire and water, they are good servants, but bad masters. - Aesop
The passion to get ahead is sometimes born of the fear lest we be left behind. - Eric Hoffer
However vast a man's spiritual resources, he is capable of but one great passion. - Blaise Pascal

Happiness

Happiness is the highest good, being a realization and perfect practice of virtue, which some can attain, while others have little or none of it. - Aristotle
He is happiest, be he king or peasant, who finds peace in his home. - Johann von Goethe
Action may not always bring happiness; but there is no happiness without action. - Benjamin Disraeli
Happiness is not a destination. It is a method of life. - Burton Hills
I have no money, no resources, no hopes. I am the happiest man alive. - Henry Miller

Life

Live as if your were to die tomorrow. Learn as if you were to live forever. - Mahatma Gandhi
Life consists not in holding good cards but in playing those you hold well. - Josh Billings
In three words I can sum up everything I've learned about life: it goes on. - Robert Frost
Believe that life is worth living and your belief will help create the fact. - Willam James
Don't take life seriously because you can't come out of it alive. - Warren Miller
